Department of Labor Apprentice Workshop

The Office of Apprenticeship Website (http://doleta.gov/OA)

The Office of Apprenticeship (OA) currently does not have a user friendly outward facing web presence to attract cutting edge industries and employers. There is a need for interactive e-tools to improve the customer experience and to create efficiencies in conducting our business.

In order for the Registered Apprenticeship system to expanded, there is a need to establish and marketing tool and a job opening clearinghouse for apprenticeships in the United States. Englandâ€™s online apprenticeship portal (www.apprenticeships.org.uk) is an example of an easy-to-navigate clearinghouse for employers and apprenticeships.

As a first step, OA would like to allow employers who are interested in sponsoring a Registered Apprenticeship program the opportunity to provide contact information via the website. The online employer enquiry page would gather contact information from potential employers. The webpage would be modelled after what is already done the United Kingdom. See: http://www.apprenticeships.org.uk/employers/employer-online-enquiry-form.aspx.

Once the information is gathered from the potential employers, the system would need to generate an automated email to inform the respondent that we have received their information and to provide basic facts and contact information about the Registered Apprenticeship program.
